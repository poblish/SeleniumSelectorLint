Best of breed accountants

Meet Steve, our Chief Accountant. With over 30 years of experience in the accounting sector, including stints at a Top 10 firm and within HMRC itself, Steve heads up our accounting operations and oversees our growing team of experts.

Under Steve’s watchful gaze, our commitment to the training and development of our accounting team has been recognised by the AAT and ACCA, two of the largest accountancy bodies in the UK. We’re also accredited by IPSE for our expertise in IR35. These accreditations recognise the investment we make in the training and development of our accounting team, seeing them through from apprentice to fully qualified accounting experts.
